Appliance Energy Consumption in Australia

Understanding how household devices impact your bill and the environment.

Why it matters?

In 2024, Australian households are facing rising energy costs. Understanding the "vampire power" (standby usage) of devices like Televisions, Fridges, and Washing Machines is crucial for reducing the national carbon footprint.

The Star Rating

Australia uses the Energy Rating Label system. The more stars, the more energy efficient. This website explores the data behind these ratings, focusing specifically on modern Televisions.

Data Source

The data presented in this visualization project is sourced from the Australian Government's Energy Rating database, covering over 5,000 appliance models.
